Mission and Vision

  • To identify and prevent inefficiency and waste in government programmes and projects arising from extravagance, fraud, and misuse of public funds.
  • To determine the extent of government’s effectiveness in the exécution of public policy as determined by legislative enactments.
  • To ensure that MDAs, elected officials and government appointees are administering the law in a fair and proper manner throughout the states of the federation.
  • To gain knowledge and understanding of governmental programmes and processes and using same to improve law making and other legislative functions.
  • To use findings from legislative oversight to improve the legislative process and to hold the executive branch accountable for good governance, including invoking necessary disciplinary measures against elected and appointed officials found wanting in the discharge of their responsibilities.
